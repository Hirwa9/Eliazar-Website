ABOUT ME

I'm thrilled to share a glimpse into my world with you. With a passion for education and a love for music, I've dedicated myself to both teaching and composing.

I strive to empower others through knowledge and learning. Whether in the classroom or one-on-one sessions, I'm committed to helping my students reach their full potential.

Besides teaching, music is my muse, and it's where I find solace and inspiration. I'm not only a music teacher but also a songwriter and composer. Crafting melodies and lyrics that resonate with others brings me immense fulfillment.

My education

I did my high school education at Petit Séminaire Saint-Aloys, Grand Séminaire Saint-Joseph de Rutongo, and Philosophicum Saint Thomas d'Aquin de Kabgayi. I graduated from the University of Technology and Arts of Byumba (UTAB) in Social Science faculty.

(UTAB), formerly known as the Institut Polytechnique de Byumba (IPB), plays a crucial role in supporting Rwanda's comprehensive development process and contributing to the country's vision of a modern, knowledge-based economy

My hobbies

In my free time, you'll often find me singing with choirs, harmonizing with fellow voices to create something magical. I also enjoy the meditative practice of playing the piano, losing myself in the melodies of classical compositions.

Music is my hobby 🤩

Engaging in choir rehearsals and participating in Holy Masses brings me great joy and peace. Singing alongside fellow choir members not only aids in prayer but also uplifts my spirit.

Personally, playing the piano serves as a means to relax and discover moments of tranquility within myself. It allows me to unwind and delve into a space of inner calm.

Listening to classical music is one of my favorite pastimes. It not only enhances my musical skills but also enriches my appreciation for the art form.
