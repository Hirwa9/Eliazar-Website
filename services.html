MY SERVICES

In my roles as an educator, tutor, and musician - songwriter and teacher, I find fulfillment in the various hats I wear.

Whether it's nurturing students' educational paths or composing soulful music, each endeavor showcases my dedication to teaching and artistic expression.

Explore the services I provide below.

Classroom Teaching and Tutoring

My students immerse themselves in vibrant learning adventures customized to meet their needs. Whether it's in a classroom environment or during individual tutoring sessions, I offer personalized guidance to nurture their academic development and boost their confidence.

Here, students engage in after-class studies where I help them review what we covered earlier in class, complete their homework, identify their weak spots, and familiarize themselves with continuous learning. This approach aims for perfection and strengthens their performance in class.

Music teaching

Discover the joy of music through interactive lessons designed just for you. Whether it's learning an instrument or exploring songwriting, I'll guide you through every step, making the journey enjoyable and rewarding.

With my music skills, I find it both interesting and fulfilling to teach others what I know. In my music class, learners and enthusiasts come to master various musical instruments. They acquire the lasting skills needed to play music independently and bring joy to their audience.

Song composition

Unleash your creativity and express yourself through the art of songwriting. With personalized guidance and support, I'll help you craft melodies and lyrics that resonate with your unique voice, turning your musical ideas into captivating compositions.

Reading musical notes (solfeggio) and writing compositions can be both fascinating and overwhelming for some musicians. Whether you want to learn or need help with writing the notes, I am here to offer my best support and services.
